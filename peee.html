Principles of Electrical and Electronics Engineering (PEEE)

Building strong foundations in electrical and electronics principles

Subject Overview

PEEE introduces students to the fundamental concepts of electrical and electronics engineering. The subject covers basic electrical circuits, DC and AC principles, electrical machines, semiconductors, and practical applications to prepare students for advanced courses and laboratory work.

Importance of PEEE

PEEE lays the groundwork for understanding how electrical and electronic systems operate. It helps students develop analytical thinking, practical problem-solving, and the ability to bridge theory with hands-on laboratory exercises.
