Privacy Policy

Non-commercial use & responsible academic sharing

Introduction

Digital Nexus respects the academic integrity and privacy of its users. This privacy policy outlines how data is handled, the non-commercial nature of the platform, and ethical expectations for all users.

Any personal information submitted for access requests is used solely for verification and providing document access. No data is sold, shared with third parties, or used for any commercial purposes.

All materials available on Digital Nexus are intended purely for academic purposes. Redistribution, resale, or commercial exploitation of any documents or templates is strictly prohibited. Violating these terms may lead to access revocation.
